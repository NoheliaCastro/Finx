Who We Are?

FINX is your personal financial assistant. We help you understand, manage, and improve your finances with simple and accessible digital tools.

To help people—especially young individuals—understand and manage their money through easy, visual, and practical tools. We want every user to gain confidence and control over their finances, reducing stress and building healthy habits.

To be the leading financial app for young people in Latin America, recognized for its education, simplicity, and personalized support. We aim to empower a generation to make smart decisions and achieve their financial goals.
